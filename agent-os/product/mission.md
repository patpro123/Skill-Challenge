# Product Mission

## Problem

Many people want to develop new skills but struggle with consistency and structure. Without a clear roadmap, curated resources, and daily accountability, most self-improvement efforts fizzle out before results are achieved.

## Target Users

Individuals who want to commit to learning a new skill over 100 days and need structure, guidance, and motivation to follow through.

## Solution

A 100-day challenge app that gives users a personalized skill roadmap, surfaces relevant learning resources, and tracks daily progress. What makes it unique is the focus on habit formation — the app actively nudges users, rewards consistency, and is designed around building a lasting practice rather than just tracking activity.
