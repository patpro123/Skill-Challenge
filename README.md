# 100-Day Skill Challenge App

A habit-formation app that guides users through a personalized 100-day learning journey.

## Problem

Most self-improvement efforts fail without structure and accountability. People who want to learn new skills lack a clear roadmap, curated resources, and daily nudges to stay consistent.

## Solution

A 100-day challenge app that gives users a personalized skill roadmap, surfaces relevant learning resources, and tracks daily progress. The focus is on habit formation — the app actively nudges users, rewards consistency, and is designed to build a lasting practice rather than just log activity.

## Target Users

Individuals who want to commit to learning a new skill over 100 days and need structure, guidance, and motivation to follow through.
